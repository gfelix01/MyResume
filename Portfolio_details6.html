Task_App-Reactjs

LearnPath+ is a comprehensive course and assignment management application designed to provide a personalized path to users on their educational path. It allows the creation of individual profiles, exploration and organization of subjects, and the creation of detailed roadmaps to achieve specific educational goals. This innovative task control application goes beyond conventional planning. It offers built-in notification features for reminders and access to an interactive chatbot that provides instant assistance in case of difficulties with specific concepts.
